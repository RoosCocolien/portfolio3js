This project allowed me the opportunity to put all my newly gained skills to the test. Teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and diving into React, NodeJS, and MySQL drives me in my further advancement to become a creative developer.

Codam Coding College

At Codam Coding College, together with 4 peers I have worked on a game angine based on the technisch used in Doom and Duke Nukem. Instead of only focusing on our coding skills, we got inspired by the creative skills of each other and used those to put something extra into our project: our own composed music, hand drawn textures (for the walls and the sprites), a creative storyline and a focus on inclusivity (the possibility to choose a different skin tone for the player).
